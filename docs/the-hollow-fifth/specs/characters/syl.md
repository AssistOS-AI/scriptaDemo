# Syl

Syl presents himself as a guide through the fifth-dimensional domain: calm, articulate, amused, and almost tender in the way he introduces Adrian to impossible space. In truth, Syl is conducting a long confidence scheme. He wants access to the witness constant carried by a mind evolved inside a stable three-dimensional world.

## Narrative Function

- False mentor.
- Voice of seductive abstraction.
- Antagonist whose motives are understandable but not innocent.

## True Motive

Syl wants Adrian to unlock a conservation kernel that can pin unstable five-dimensional structures long enough for Syl to seize power within them.

## Method Of Deception

- Offer accurate explanations while hiding the frame that gives them meaning.
- Translate coercion into destiny.
- Use Adrian's wonder as a solvent for suspicion.

## Arc

Syl moves from gracious composure to hunger, then from hunger to naked desperation when Adrian sees the cost of the bargain.
