# Adrian Vale

Adrian Vale is the protagonist, a machine learning researcher with enough mathematical intuition to notice when a world is lying by being too elegant. His greatest strength is that he can convert bewilderment into questions. His greatest weakness is that explanation feels like safety to him, which makes him vulnerable to a deceptive guide.

## Narrative Function

- Primary point of view.
- Carrier of the bridge between ML geometry and cosmological stability.
- Moral center who must decide whether compassion justifies surrendering autonomy.

## Desire

Adrian wants orientation, then truth, then a way to return without losing the knowledge he gained.

## False Belief

He initially believes that better explanation always means deeper honesty.

## Transformation

He learns that some limits are merciful and that refusal can be a more ethical form of intelligence than total comprehension.
