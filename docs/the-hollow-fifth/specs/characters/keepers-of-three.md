# Keepers of Three

The Keepers of Three are a collective intelligence that tends the boundaries between stable worlds and extravagant ones. They are not benevolent in the sentimental sense, but they are guardians of the harsh arithmetic that allows matter, memory, and life to persist.

## Narrative Function

- Reveal the hidden stakes.
- Give cosmic scale to Adrian's personal decision.
- Articulate the ethical side of conservation.

## Ethic

Their ethic is severe: not every realm deserves stabilization, and not every hunger justifies a bridge.
