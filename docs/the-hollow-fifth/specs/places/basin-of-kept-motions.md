# Basin of Kept Motions

The Basin of Kept Motions is a vast depression where trajectories glow without dispersing, as if conservation laws have been gathered there and held under watch. It is the most overtly cosmological place in the book. Here the stakes expand from one person to the architecture of viable universes.

## Function In Story

- Houses the revelation of Syl's plan.
- Makes the physics philosophy explicit.
- Stages the moral break between comprehension and consent.
