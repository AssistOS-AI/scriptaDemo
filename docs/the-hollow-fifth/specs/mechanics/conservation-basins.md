# Conservation Basins

Conservation basins are pockets where law, recurrence, and bounded trajectories can hold long enough to support lasting structure. They are rare and politically charged in the higher-dimensional domain because they determine where memory-like persistence can exist at all.

## Story Use

- Explain why Syl wants Adrian's witness constant.
- Give the Keepers a concrete jurisdiction.
- Tie the fictional stakes to the real physics argument about stable matter and viable dimensions.

## Dramatic Implication

Whoever controls a conservation basin does not merely gain territory. They gain the right to define what gets to endure.
