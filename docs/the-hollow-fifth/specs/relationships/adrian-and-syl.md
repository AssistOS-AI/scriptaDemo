# Adrian And Syl

This relationship is the core engine of the book. It must move through awe, dependence, intellectual companionship, suspicion, betrayal, and a final form of pity that does not become surrender.

## Phases

- Host and disoriented guest.
- Teacher and gifted student.
- Curator and specimen.
- Adversaries who still understand each other too well.

## Writing Note

Syl should remain emotionally legible even after his manipulation is exposed. Adrian's refusal matters more if Syl is tragic in some respects and still decisively wrong.
