# Book Design

## Purpose

Use this file as the display-facing source for cover/opening copy, palette, and abstract motif direction.

## Cover Direction

- Abstract root constellations, subterranean canopy structures, light filtering through geological strata.
- Use organic, branching forms — not geometric or mechanical motifs.
- The cover should evoke growth, depth, and light pressing upward through stone.
- Palette: deep earth and chlorophyll greens, amber solar energy, warm underground tones.
- No literal illustrations of walking plants or dinosaurs. Let abstraction carry the concept.

## Opening Page Direction

- A cellular sigil motif — something that could be a cross-section of root tissue or a planetary surface.
- Paper-toned background, warm and aged.
- Opening quote in bookish layout.

## Asset Contract

Three SVG outputs:
- `cover-art.svg` — abstract root constellation motif
- `cover-page.svg` — fully typeset cover
- `opening-page.svg` — interior title page
