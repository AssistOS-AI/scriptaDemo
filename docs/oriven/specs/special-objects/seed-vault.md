# The Descender

The Descender is Caelith's probe — a biological-crystalline construct designed to survive atmospheric transit from Venus to Earth and return data. It is the first Oriveni-made object sent beyond their own atmosphere.

Built from surface-adapted materials (Caelith's specialty), powered by concentrated solar energy, and guided by crystalline computation arrays, the Descender represents both Oriveni technological achievement and ethical transgression. It was launched without full root-network consensus, over Verath's formal objection.

The Descender transmits data through modulated light pulses received by the Origaya Lenses and distributed through the root-network. Its transmissions confirmed that predation on Origaya is systemic — not a single disturbing observation but a comprehensive pattern of life killing life to survive.
