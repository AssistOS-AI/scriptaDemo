# Character Emotional Arcs

## Aethon

- **Start**: Deep contentment, the quiet confidence of four thousand years of unbroken precedent
- **Midpoint**: Silent crisis — no precedent, no guidance, the archive empty when it is needed most
- **End**: Renewed commitment, but conscious rather than assumed. Ethics chosen despite knowledge of its contingency

## Lithos

- **Start**: Analytical contentment, the satisfaction of processing data without emotional interference
- **Midpoint**: Growing isolation — her detachment is accurate but costs her connection to the network
- **End**: Accepted as necessary — not loved, not forgiven, but recognized as indispensable

## Verath

- **Start**: Protective certainty, the confidence of enforcing a law that is both moral and biological
- **Midpoint**: Desperation — the law is being questioned, and his identity is built on enforcement
- **End**: Pragmatic acceptance — the law still holds, but now it holds by choice rather than by nature

## Mirel

- **Start**: Wide-eyed wonder at the universe beyond Oriven, then the first shadow of horror
- **Midpoint**: Wounded witness — she cannot stop watching, even as watching damages her root-connections
- **End**: Understanding observer — still watching, but with eyes that comprehend rather than flinch

## Caelith

- **Start**: Restless energy, the desire to break boundaries and reach beyond observation
- **Midpoint**: Heresy becoming action, the thrill and danger of transgression
- **End**: Constrained but not stopped — her signal array is unbuilt, but the possibility of it has changed everything
