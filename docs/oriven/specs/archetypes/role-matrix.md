# Archetype Role Matrix

| Role | Archetype | Character | Core Drive |
|------|-----------|-----------|------------|
| Protagonist | Exile Heir | Aethon | Protect and restore the ethical inheritance |
| Counterpart | Cold Strategist | Lithos | Turn uncertainty into understanding through analysis |
| Pressure Source | Civic Guardian | Verath | Keep the civilization stable, suppress threats to order |
| Witness | Wounded Seeker | Mirel | Make suffering legible through relentless observation |
| Catalyst | Heretic Maker | Caelith | Break boundaries to discover what lies beyond |

## Interaction Pattern

- Aethon provides legitimacy and precedent. When he falters, the network falters.
- Lithos provides analytical clarity. When she is right in ways that feel wrong, she forces the network to confront uncomfortable truths.
- Verath provides stability. When stability becomes suppression, he becomes the enemy of necessary growth.
- Mirel provides evidence. Her observations are the foundation of every argument.
- Caelith provides action. When observation and analysis stall, she pushes toward contact.

## Tensions

- Aethon needs Lithos's analysis but fears its conclusions.
- Verath needs Aethon's authority but cannot accept his growing uncertainty.
- Mirel needs Caelith's boldness but fears its consequences.
- Lithos needs Mirel's observations but treats them as data rather than experience.
- Caelith needs Mirel's legitimacy but cannot accept her horror.
