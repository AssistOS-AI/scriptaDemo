# Aethon and Verath

## Dynamic

Verath was once Aethon's student in the deep root-protocols. Their relationship carries the weight of a mentor who has seen his student become the enforcer of the very principles the mentor now questions.

## Tension

Verath believes in the absolute law: no killing, no compromise with killing, no understanding of killing. Aethon's growing uncertainty threatens not just the law but Verath's entire identity. If the law is local, Verath has spent a millennium enforcing a convenience.

## Escalation

As the crisis deepens, Verath moves from caution to suppression to attempted disconnection of the Origaya observers. Aethon must decide whether Verath's commitment to stability is still protecting the network or whether it has become the thing that threatens it most.
