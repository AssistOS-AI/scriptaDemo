# Mirel and Caelith

## Dynamic

Mirel documents Origaya through observation. Caelith wants to reach it directly. Mirel's careful data becomes Caelith's justification for contact. They need each other — Mirel's legitimacy gives Caelith's transgression intellectual cover, and Caelith's boldness gives Mirel's observation a sense of direction.

## Tension

Mirel is horrified by origayan predation but cannot stop watching. Caelith is not horrified — she is fascinated. Mirel feels that Caelith's fascination dishonors the suffering she observes. Caelith feels that Mirel's horror is sentimental and blocks understanding.

## Escalation

When Caelith uses Mirel's observations to justify the probe and then the signal array, Mirel must confront the fact that honest documentation can be weaponized. Observation and contact are not as different as she wants them to be.
