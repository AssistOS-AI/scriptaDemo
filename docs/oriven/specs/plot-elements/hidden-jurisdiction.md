# Probe Descent

Caelith's launch of the Descender probe toward Origaya. This is the first deliberate physical contact with a predatory world. It is a heresy against caution, a breach of protocol, and an act of intellectual courage — or recklessness, depending on who in the network you ask._VERATH_OBJECTION and the formal root-network debate that follows define the Second Act.
