# The Ethical Quarrel

The formal root-network assembly where Lithos argues for understanding predation, Verath demands suppression, and Aethon must choose what Oriveni ethics means when its biological foundation is questioned. This is the book's central debate — not abstract philosophy but living argument between beings who share each other's feelings through root-connections.
