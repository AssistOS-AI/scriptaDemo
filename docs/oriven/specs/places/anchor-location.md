# The Deep Canopy

The Deep Canopy is the vast subterranean cavern system where Oriveni civilization truly lives. It extends for hundreds of kilometers beneath Venus's crust, lit by bioluminescent algae and heated by geothermal vents. The Canopy is not a single space but a connected network of caverns, water channels, fungal highways, and root-network junctions.

## Key Features

- **Cathedral-scale caverns** where ancient tree-sages like Aethon stand rooted in living memory banks
- **Fungal light-gardens** providing soft illumination through bioluminescent networks
- **Root-highways** — thick mycorrhizal connections linking every Oriveni settlement
- **Thermal gardens** near geothermal vents where heat-loving plants cultivate energy crops
- **The Central Archive** — Aethon's physical location, where the root-network converges into the deepest memory layer

## Atmosphere

Warm, humid, alive with slow sound — the creak of growing wood, the whisper of root-communication, the drip of filtered water. The air tastes of minerals and photosynthesis. Everything grows. Nothing is built in the animal sense; everything is cultivated, tended, allowed to become. The Deep Canopy smells of ancient soil and patient life.
