# The Probe Chamber

A specialized cavern near the surface where Caelith designed and constructed the Descender — the probe sent toward Origaya. It serves as both laboratory and moral battleground, the place where theory becomes action and where the debate over contact vs. observation becomes most acute.

## Key Features

- **The Construction Cradle** — where the Descender was grown from crystalline substrates and biological materials
- **The Launch Shaft** — a geological formation leading to the surface, used to send the probe through Venus's atmosphere
- **The Data Return Terminal** — the root-network junction where the Descender's transmissions are received and decoded
- **The Witness Circle** — root-connections from observers across the deepCanopy who link to the Chamber when data returns

## Atmosphere

Tense, electric with root-network debate, humming with potential. The Probe Chamber is where ideas become actions and where the Oriveni ethical framework faces its most direct challenge. Every surface is marked by the growth-patterns of Caelith's construction — urgent, branching, competitive.
