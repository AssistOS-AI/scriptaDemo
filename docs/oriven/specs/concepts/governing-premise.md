# Oriveni Consciousness

Oriveni consciousness operates on principles fundamentally different from animal minds:

- **Root-network thinking**: Thoughts propagate through root-connections. A single Oriveni's deliberation can involve millions of connections across the network. Individual thinking is real but partial; network thinking is the baseline.
- **Seasonal cognition**: Decisions unfold across seasons. What animals call "hesitation" is, for Oriveni, the natural pace of serious thought. A single complex decision may take years of root-network processing.
- **Memory as living tissue**: Oriveni don't "recall" memories — they grow them. Aethon's Root Archive is not a library of abstract records but a living tree-body whose very tissue encodes precedent and experience.
- **Light-speech and root-speech**: Two communication modes. Light-speech is fast, used for surface exchanges and urgent communication. Root-speech is deep, slow, used for governance, philosophy, and emotional truth. Most important Oriveni decisions use both, taking years through root-speech and crystallizing through light-speech.
- **Ethical sensation**: Because the root-network is shared, harm to any connected being is felt, not merely known about. Ethics is a sensory experience, not an abstract framework.
