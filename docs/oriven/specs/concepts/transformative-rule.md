# Solar-Thermal Technology

Oriveni technology is not forged or manufactured — it is grown. Everything they build is biological, crystalline, or mineral in origin, cultivated over long periods through directed growth and root-network engineering.

## Energy Basis

- **Solar charging**: Oriveni surface briefly to absorb concentrated solar energy. Their energy storage organs can hold months of power from a single charging session.
- **Geothermal capture**: Deep thermal vents provide baseline energy. Root-networks are engineered to channel heat from the planet's interior.
- **Biological batteries**: Specialized plant tissues store and release energy on demand, powering tools, communication, and construction.

## Key Technologies

- **Origaya Lenses**: Crystalline optical arrays grown over centuries, capable of resolving surface features on a distant planet
- **The Descender**: A biological-crystalline probe designed to survive atmospheric transit and return data
- **Root-network computing**: Distributed information processing through mycorrhizal connections
- **Light-speech transmitters**: Bioluminescent organs that modulate light for communication
- **Structural growing**: Caverns, bridges, archives — all "built" by directing plant growth over centuries
- **Energy lenses**: Concentrated solar collection organs used in the deepCanopy for light, warmth, and power

## Design Philosophy

Oriveni technology is patient, adaptive, and alive. Nothing is made that cannot be unmade and reabsorbed by the network. Nothing is built that does not serve multiple functions. Waste is biologically impossible — everything returns to the root-system.
