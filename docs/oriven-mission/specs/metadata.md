## Crucial Corrections from Volume 1

- Origaya (Earth) orbits the same star as Oriven (Venus) but at a greater distance, receiving less solar energy per unit area. This is why Origaya's life evolved predation: energy scarcity makes killing thermodynamically necessary, while Oriven's solar abundance makes sharing the efficient strategy.
- The star appears smaller from Origaya not because it is a different star, but because Origaya is farther away.
- All five original characters (Aethon, Mirel, Lithos, Verath, Caelith) survive into Volume 2 through bio-technological life extension. They are now effectively immortal — preserved by a civilization that has never had to face the death of an individual in remembered history.
