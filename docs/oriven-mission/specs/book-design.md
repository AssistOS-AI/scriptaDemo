# Book Design

## Purpose

Use this file as the display-facing source for cover/opening copy, palette, and abstract motif direction.

## Visual Direction

- The palette shifts from Volume 1's deep earth greens to Volume 2's cosmic blues and teals, reflecting the expansion beyond the planet.
- The cover should suggest both the vastness of interplanetary distance and the intimacy of a root-connection — cosmic scale and biological closeness in tension.
- Abstract motifs: orbital paths, root-network branching (now extended to cosmic scale), the double helix of Oriveni-Origayan fusion.
- No literal illustrations of ships, dinosaurs, or fused beings. Let abstraction and color carry the concept.

## Opening Page Direction

- The opening page echoes Volume 1's cellular sigil but with an additional orbital ring — suggesting that what was once contained within Oriven is now reaching outward.
- Use the same paper tone but with a slightly cooler cast, indicating the temperature of space versus the warmth of the deepCanopy.

## Asset Contract

Three SVG outputs:
- `cover-art.svg` — abstract orbital-root constellation motif
- `cover-page.svg` — fully typeset cover
- `opening-page.svg` — interior title page
