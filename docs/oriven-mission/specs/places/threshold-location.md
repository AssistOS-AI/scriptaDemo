# Threshold Location

## Narrative Role

Crossing point where the governing rules become visible.

## Sensory Logic

Describe what this place feels like to enter, navigate, and survive.

## World Building Hooks

- Threshold Geography: The world is organized around crossings, gates, layers, or liminal zones.
- Layered Jurisdictions: Authority overlaps, conflicts, and persists in partial forms.
- Ritualized Law: Procedure is inseparable from symbol and legitimacy.

## Scene Use

- What kind of scene belongs here.
- What kind of pressure intensifies here.
- What changes once a character returns here later in the story.
