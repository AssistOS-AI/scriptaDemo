# Character Evolution

## Global Evolution Thesis

The protagonist should end the book with a different relationship to order, responsibility, and truth than at the opening.

## Role-Based Tracks

- **Protagonist**: Opening posture shaped by Witness Scribe. Midpoint pressure should expose the archetype's shadow. Ending should either transform or fully reveal that shadow.
- **Counterpart**: Opening posture shaped by Threshold Guide. Midpoint pressure should expose the archetype's shadow. Ending should either transform or fully reveal that shadow.
- **Pressure Source**: Opening posture shaped by Grief Bearer. Midpoint pressure should expose the archetype's shadow. Ending should either transform or fully reveal that shadow.
- **Witness**: Opening posture shaped by Exile Heir. Midpoint pressure should expose the archetype's shadow. Ending should either transform or fully reveal that shadow.
- **Catalyst**: Opening posture shaped by Wounded Seeker. Midpoint pressure should expose the archetype's shadow. Ending should either transform or fully reveal that shadow.

## Structural Check

- Chapter 1 should establish each major role's usable mask.
- Middle chapters should turn that mask into a liability or strategic advantage.
- Final chapters should force each major role into a choice that proves what they actually serve.
