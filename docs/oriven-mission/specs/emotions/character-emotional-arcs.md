# Character Emotional Arcs

## Emotional Spine

- Opening emotion: uncertainty under pressure.
- Midpoint emotion: sharpened attachment mixed with distrust.
- Late-book emotion: clarity that costs comfort.
- Ending emotion: transformed commitment rather than simple relief.

## Role Reminders

- **Protagonist** should experience pressure through the lens of Witness Scribe.
- **Counterpart** should experience pressure through the lens of Threshold Guide.
- **Pressure Source** should experience pressure through the lens of Grief Bearer.
- **Witness** should experience pressure through the lens of Exile Heir.
- **Catalyst** should experience pressure through the lens of Wounded Seeker.
