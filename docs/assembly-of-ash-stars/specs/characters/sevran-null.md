# Sevran Null

Sevran Null is an archivist-smuggler descended from the Null Basilica, a civilization of probability ascetics who believed truth was clearest in the zones where prediction fails. He survives by trafficking access, routes, old signatures, and partial laws between stronger powers. He is brilliant, evasive, charming in a dry and dangerous way, and almost always holding one more card than he has admitted.

## Narrative Function

- Rogue third angle in the central coalition.
- Specialist in dead infrastructure and forbidden access paths.
- Carrier of betrayal risk who never becomes random.

## True Need

Sevran wants a future in which optionality survives. He fears both Varkast's empire and any orderly settlement rigid enough to leave no room for people like him.

## Arc

He begins as a seller of leverage. He ends by risking his own escape routes to prevent the final compression of civilizational difference.
