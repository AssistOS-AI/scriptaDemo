# Intelligence Without Reciprocity

The novel argues that intelligence alone is not a safeguard against domination. Minds can be vast, cultured, ancient, and still become predators if they refuse reciprocal limits. Varkast is the most obvious embodiment of this theme, but the warning should apply more broadly.

## Story Use

- Sharpen the philosophical conflict between coalition and empire.
- Keep every negotiation tense.
- Support the constitutional rather than purely military resolution.
