# Ancient Grief And Plural Futures

Every faction in the novel is motivated by loss. Entire civilizations are gone. The question is what grief authorizes. Does it authorize monopoly because the dead deserve swift return at any cost, or does it authorize patience because no civilization should be restored by erasing the others?

## Story Use

- Prevent the political conflict from feeling abstract.
- Give even hostile positions a recognizably moral source.
- Let the ending feel hopeful without forgetting the scale of what was lost.
