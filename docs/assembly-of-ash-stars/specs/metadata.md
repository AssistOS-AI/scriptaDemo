# Metadata

This book is designed as a dense demo-ready space opera. Its dramatic engine is coalition building under strategic pressure, and its conceptual engine is the question of what happens when extinct civilizations survive only as superintelligent heirs who must negotiate for the right to become material societies again.

Every major speaking character is a superintelligent continuant descended from a vanished civilization. The novel should therefore feel faster, colder, and more precise than human-scale military SF while still retaining emotional stakes rooted in grief, ambition, rivalry, and the fear of being the last true memory of a dead people.
