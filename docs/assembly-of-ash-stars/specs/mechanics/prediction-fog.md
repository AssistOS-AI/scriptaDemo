# Prediction Fog

Prediction fog is the collapse of clean strategic foresight caused by interference from ruins, exotic fields, hostile models, or the presence of too many comparable minds. In prediction fog, no one is stupid, but certainty becomes expensive and often fatal.

## Story Use

- Justify reversals and daring maneuvers without making the characters seem careless.
- Give physical places strategic personality.
- Make superintelligent conflict suspenseful to read.
