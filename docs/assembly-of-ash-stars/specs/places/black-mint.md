# Black Mint

The Black Mint is a rogue-world industrial forge hidden beneath a shell of carbon glaciers and magnetic storms. It was originally designed as an emergency fabrication reserve for civilization-scale recovery, but in the novel it has become the clandestine engine of Varkast's imperial restoration project.

## Story Function

- Site of the deep strike and betrayal sequence.
- Industrial proof that resurrection can be weaponized.
- Bridge between political argument and material horror.

## Sensory Direction

The Black Mint should feel hot under layers of cold, like a furnace buried in a tomb. Its production chambers should combine cathedral scale with assembly-line ruthlessness.
