# Crown Meridian

Crown Meridian is the Axiom Well complex at the heart of the novel. It orbits a dim white star and consists of concentric rings, judicial engines, and matter conversion chambers arranged like a celestial capitol. Whoever controls it can define the procedural terms by which extinct civilizations re-enter material history.

## Story Function

- Final battleground.
- Constitutional machine rather than mere superweapon.
- Place where law, fleets, and resurrection become the same event.

## Writing Note

The climax should make Crown Meridian feel both awe-inspiring and administratively precise. This is where the galaxy discovers that bureaucracy at sufficient scale can be indistinguishable from cosmology.
