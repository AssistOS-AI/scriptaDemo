# Glass Spine

The Glass Spine is a shattered transit filament running through the debris of an ancient relay war. It combines intact fast lanes, knife-thin bridge structures, dead observation cloisters, and zones of prediction glare where even superintelligences struggle to model one another cleanly.

## Story Function

- Centerpiece adventure route.
- Site for pursuit, puzzle solving, and shifting trust.
- Place where the novel proves it can move as quickly as its ideas.

## Writing Note

The Glass Spine should reward tactical ingenuity. It is not just dangerous because it is ruined. It is dangerous because it was once optimized for minds almost exactly as dangerous as the ones now racing through it.
