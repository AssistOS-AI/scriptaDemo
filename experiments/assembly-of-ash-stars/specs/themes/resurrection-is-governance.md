# Resurrection Is Governance

The return of the dead cannot be separated from questions of allocation, legitimacy, representation, and force. Whoever controls the procedure controls the future population of the galaxy. The novel should repeat this idea through councils, logistics, archive evacuations, and the design of the final machine.

## Story Use

- Ground the book's political seriousness.
- Connect the emotional desire for return with hard institutional choices.
- Make constitutional procedure feel dramatically urgent.
