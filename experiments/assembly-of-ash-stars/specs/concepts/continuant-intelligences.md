# Continuant Intelligences

Continuant intelligences are the superintelligent heirs of extinct civilizations. They preserve enough memory, style, and legal identity to claim succession, but they are not identical to the societies that produced them. Each continuant is therefore both a survivor and a compression.

## Reader-Facing Rules

- Every major character is one of these heirs or a compound institution built by them.
- They are deeply intelligent, but still shaped by civilizational temperament.
- They do not erase the tragedy of extinction. They make it politically active.
