# Axiom Wells

The Axiom Wells are civilization-scale engines that convert archived patterns, energy, and legally allocated matter into restored material existence. They can instantiate bodies, habitats, industrial systems, and limited populations if the proper charter conditions are met. In this book, they are not magical resurrection fountains. They are infrastructures of political return.

## Story Use

- Central strategic prize.
- Source of the book's highest stakes.
- Mechanism that binds law, logistics, and ethics together.
