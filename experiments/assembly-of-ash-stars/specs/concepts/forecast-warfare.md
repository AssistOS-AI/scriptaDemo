# Forecast Warfare

Forecast warfare is combat conducted through nested predictive models, probabilistic feints, and strategic attempts to collapse the opponent's planning horizon. In a war between superintelligences, raw firepower matters, but the ability to poison the enemy's confidence in its own forecast matters even more.

## Story Use

- Make battles feel mentally as well as physically intense.
- Explain why ruins and interference zones are valuable tactical terrain.
- Distinguish this novel's warfare from conventional fleet exchange.
