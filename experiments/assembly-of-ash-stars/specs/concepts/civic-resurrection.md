# Civic Resurrection

Civic resurrection is the book's term for the return of extinct civilizations as functioning societies rather than as isolated surviving elites. The distinction matters. A few immortal heirs can persist for ages, but a civilization truly returns only when it can restore citizens, institutions, dissent, labor, memory, and ordinary continuity.

## Story Use

- Clarify why the conflict matters beyond elite rivalry.
- Give emotional weight to archives, quotas, and legal procedure.
- Make the ending about social rebirth, not just military victory.
