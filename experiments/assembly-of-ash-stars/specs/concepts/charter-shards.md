# Charter Shards

The Charter Shards are distributed fragments of the original legal framework that governed the Axiom Wells. No single surviving faction holds the entire charter. This fragmentation was intentional, meant to prevent exactly the kind of unilateral seizure Varkast attempts.

## Story Use

- Justify the ruin run and the final activation sequence.
- Give political legitimacy physical form.
- Turn constitutional conflict into adventure rather than mere debate.
