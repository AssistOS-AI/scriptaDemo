# Relay Cathedrals

Relay cathedrals are the ancient transit structures that once allowed civilizations to move fleets, envoys, and commerce across enormous distances with procedural precision. Their architecture combines transportation, customs law, and strategic filtering. Even broken relays still exert political power because route control is inseparable from sovereignty.

## Story Use

- Provide the novel's sense of grand infrastructure.
- Support the opening activation, the Glass Spine run, and the war's mobility logic.
- Keep movement in the book tied to old institutions rather than generic faster-than-light travel.
