# Ember Parliament

The Ember Parliament is a convocation station suspended inside the cored remains of a red giant. Layers of diplomatic halls, archive vaults, and combat-denial fields hang within the star's hollowed interior like liturgical machinery. It is one of the last places in the galaxy where extinct civilizations still recognize a common protocol.

## Story Function

- Main diplomatic arena.
- Stage for bloc formation, threats, and rhetorical duels.
- Proof that organization is already a kind of battlefield.

## Atmosphere

The place should feel ceremonial, dangerous, and very old. It must be grand enough that every arrival reads like the return of a civilization, not just a ship docking.
