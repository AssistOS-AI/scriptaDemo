# Oryx Gate

Oryx Gate is a dormant relay cathedral at the edge of surveyed space. Its vast ring is broken in several places, and its maintenance intelligences have long since decayed into ritualized fragments. When it reactivates in the opening chapter, the event feels less like a machine reboot and more like a dead jurisdiction remembering its authority.

## Story Function

- Opening mystery site.
- First proof that the old galactic order may be returning.
- Launch point for the entire strategic conflict.

## Visual Direction

Use black space, copper relay light, and immense negative volume. The first activation should feel ceremonial and predatory at once.
