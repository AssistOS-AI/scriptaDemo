# Ossuary Front

The Ossuary Front is a war theater formed from white Dyson fragments, dead orbital habitats, and preserved archive vaults left adrift after a civilization-scale collapse. The place is both battlefield and graveyard. To fight there is to fire through the architecture of the dead.

## Story Function

- Major fleet conflict zone.
- Moral test because civilian archives and industrial reserves occupy the same space.
- Physical reminder of what failed resurrection or renewed imperial war could produce.

## Writing Note

The action here should be fast and precise, but the ruin field must keep grief visible in the background of every tactical choice.
