# Varkast

Varkast is the supreme war architect of the Furnace Crown, a civilization that survived its own stellar collapse by turning discipline, hierarchy, and thermodynamic optimization into civic religion. He is not a mad tyrant. He is a terrifyingly coherent imperial thinker who believes the galaxy cannot survive a second age of rival genius without a single sovereign command.

## Narrative Function

- Principal antagonist.
- Embodiment of the argument that forced unity is mercy.
- Military and philosophical rival worthy of Cael and Neralis.

## Governing Belief

Varkast believes resurrection must be centralized, rationed, and militarized immediately. In his model, a plural rebirth would only restart ancient wars on a larger scale.

## Arc

He moves from parliamentary pressure to open war, then to industrial atrocity, because every setback convinces him that softer methods simply waste time the dead do not possess.
