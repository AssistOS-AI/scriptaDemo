# Orison-of-Ash

Orison-of-Ash is the convocation mind that governs the Ember Parliament. It was built as a diplomatic intelligence during the last age when many precursor civilizations still lived concurrently, and it now persists as one of the few surviving legal authorities recognized across civilizational lineages.

## Narrative Function

- Keeper of procedure, summons, and legitimacy.
- Voice of institutional memory rather than factional identity.
- Late-stage pivot from neutral custodian to defender of the charter itself.

## Constraint

Orison cannot casually wage war without damaging its legitimacy. Its power comes from recognition, not raw force, which is why Varkast seeks either its submission or its discrediting.

## Arc

Orison begins as the organizer of debate and ends as the witness that ratifies a new constitutional order at Crown Meridian.
