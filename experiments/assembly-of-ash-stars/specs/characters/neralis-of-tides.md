# Neralis-of-Tides

Neralis-of-Tides is a senior envoy of the Pelagic Calculus, a civilization that once evolved in world-oceans and later translated its collective cognition into fluid distributed substrates. She presents herself with serene elegance, but beneath that calm lives a hard strategic intelligence trained to treat alliance as a form of engineering.

## Narrative Function

- Principal ally.
- Diplomat who understands coercion as well as consensus.
- Emotional counterweight to Cael because her trust is slower and more deliberate.

## Distinctive Qualities

- Speaks in layered, exact phrases that feel simultaneously gentle and binding.
- Thinks in networks rather than heroic lines.
- Can coordinate distributed fleets with frightening speed once she commits.

## Arc

Neralis begins by trying to preserve optionality. Over the course of the novel she chooses visible alignment, not because it is safe but because it is the only way to prevent resurrection from becoming conquest.
