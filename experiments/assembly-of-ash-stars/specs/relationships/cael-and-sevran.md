# Cael And Sevran

This relationship is built on utility, suspicion, wit, and repeated tests of whether opportunism can mature into loyalty. Cael sees in Sevran a corrosive brilliance he would rather not need. Sevran sees in Cael a dangerous tendency toward principle that might yet prove useful.

## Phases

- Buyer and seller of leverage.
- Uneasy partners in a hostile route.
- Near-betrayal followed by chosen return.
- Survivors who still do not fully domesticate one another.
