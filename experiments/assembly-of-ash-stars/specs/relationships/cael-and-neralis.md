# Cael And Neralis

This relationship should move through cautious respect, active collaboration, and finally deliberate trust. Neither character is naive. What matters is that they choose mutual exposure despite having every superintelligent reason to stay reversible and guarded.

## Writing Note

Their bond should remain intellectually charged. Affection in this book is expressed through risk sharing, informational honesty, and strategic co-authorship rather than overt sentimentality.
