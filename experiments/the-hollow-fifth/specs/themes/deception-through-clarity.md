# Deception Through Clarity

Syl does not manipulate Adrian through nonsense. He manipulates him through elegant partial truth. The theme insists that explanation itself can become an instrument of capture when the frame is selectively withheld.

## Narrative Function

- Make the antagonist intellectually credible.
- Keep the reader almost aligned with Syl long enough for the betrayal to matter.
- Distinguish lying from strategic illumination.

## Signals To Use

- Syl often says accurate things too early, with suspicious smoothness.
- Adrian's trust rises whenever his confusion decreases.
- The story should repeatedly ask whether understanding implies consent.
