# Constraint Is Mercy

One of the book's central claims is that limitation is not merely what reality suffers; it is what allows reality to become livable. Boundaries create repetition, repetition creates trust, and trust permits both science and love.

## Narrative Function

- Prevent the philosophy from sounding like abstract resignation.
- Reframe finitude as a gift with costs, not a prison with decorations.
- Make Adrian's refusal feel protective rather than fearful.

## Scenes That Must Express It

- Adrian learning to search for persistence lines rather than infinite possibilities.
- The Keepers explaining why stable law requires disciplined dimensionality.
- The final return to ordinary mornings, now understood as merciful rather than dull.
