# Recurrence And Identity

The witness constant is not a supernatural gem hidden in the hero. It is the identity forged by repeated life inside a stable world. Recurrence is how a person becomes legible to themselves across time, and the book should treat that as both scientifically suggestive and emotionally sacred.

## Narrative Function

- Connect memory, conservation, and personal ethics.
- Give Mira's presence structural weight rather than nostalgic decoration.
- Turn ordinary life into the book's final source of awe.
