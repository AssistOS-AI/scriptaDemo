# Stable Matter In Three Dimensions

The novella proposes that a life-bearing universe needs more than abundance; it needs disciplined dimensionality. In too many spatial dimensions, inverse-square style stability breaks down, orbits fail to stay gracefully bounded, and matter loses the reliable patterns from which chemistry and memory emerge.

## Story Use

- Turns physics into an ethical argument for limits.
- Explains why Adrian's human continuity is valuable to Syl.
- Grounds the ending in a positive vision of finitude.
