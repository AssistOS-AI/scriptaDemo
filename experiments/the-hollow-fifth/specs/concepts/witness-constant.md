# Witness Constant

The witness constant is the book's fictional term for the continuity carried by a consciousness shaped inside a stable low-dimensional universe. It is not raw intelligence. It is the capacity to keep identity through recurrence, to recognize the same law after time has passed, and to remain oneself across changing conditions.

## Story Use

- Object of Syl's manipulation.
- Bridge between personal memory and cosmic conservation.
- Device that converts abstract philosophy into dramatic leverage.
