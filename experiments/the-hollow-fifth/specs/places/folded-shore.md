# Folded Shore

The Folded Shore looks like a coast made of repeated edges of the same sea. Waves arrive from neighboring histories and collapse into one another before touching land. It is the ideal final place because it feels like a border, a return path, and a meditation on choosing one life out of many.

## Function In Story

- Provides the emotional and visual resolution.
- Lets Adrian choose finitude without making it feel like defeat.
- Leaves Syl with the image of a freedom he could not accept because it came with limits.
