# Prism Threshold

The Prism Threshold is the first place Adrian consciously occupies after his displacement. It feels like a corridor made of overlapping decisions, where every surface contains the faint angle of another possible approach. The place should communicate wonder before it communicates danger.

## Function In Story

- Establishes five-dimensional perception through sensation rather than theory.
- Lets Syl appear as a host before he is recognized as a manipulator.
- Gives the story its first image of adjacency without stable distance.
