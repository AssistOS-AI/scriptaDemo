# Annular Library

The Annular Library is a ringed archive whose shelves curve through causal neighborhoods instead of physical aisles. Books seem close and unreachable at once because proximity no longer guarantees retrieval. The location embodies the machine learning problem of sparse neighborhoods in high dimension.

## Function In Story

- Turns abstract geometry into architecture.
- Gives Syl room to teach while revealing his taste for curated truths.
- Lets Adrian connect his academic intuition to lived experience.
