# Mira Vale

Mira Vale is Adrian's sister, present mostly through memory, voice, and the habits of thought she helped form in him. She is not a passive sentimental anchor. She represents a disciplined affection for the physical world: weather, weight, recurring seasons, and the human scale of promise.

## Narrative Function

- Emotional counterweight to Syl's abstraction.
- Reminder that love is made of repetition rather than transcendence.
- The memory that stabilizes Adrian when the fifth-dimensional world tries to diffuse his identity.

## Effect On Adrian

When Adrian remembers Mira, he does not become less intelligent. He becomes less manipulable.
